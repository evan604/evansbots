# The Hidden Costs of Entrepreneurship

## Blog Post (Highland) - Draft

### Hook
Being your own boss has costs. Some obvious. Some not. Here's what they don't tell you.

### Outline

**1. Financial Costs**
- No benefits
- No retirement match
- Health insurance
- Taxes

**2. Time Costs**
- No off switch
- Always on
- Weekend work
- Missed events

**3. Emotional Costs**
- Stress
- Loneliness
- Isolation
- Responsibility

**4. Opportunity Costs**
- Salary you didn't earn
- Experience you didn't get
- Skills you didn't learn

**5. The Offset**
- Control
- Upside
- Flexibility
- Purpose

### Word count target: 800-1000 words
