# The Hiring Framework for Growing Businesses

## Blog Post (Highland) - Draft

### Hook
When to hire? Who to hire first? Here's the hiring framework for businesses scaling up.

### Outline

**1. The Signs**
- Overworked team
- Missing skills
- Growth stalled
- Quality drops

**2. The Order**
- Revenue roles first
- Operations second
- Support third
- Management last

**3. Hire for**
- Skills
- Culture fit
- Growth potential
- Coachability

**4. The Process**
- Clear job description
- Structured interviews
- Skills assessment
- Reference checks

**5. Onboarding**
- Clear expectations
- Training plan
- 30/60/90 days
- Regular check-ins

🚩 Missing skills
🚩 Growth stalled
🚩 Quality drops

1️⃣ Revenue roles first

3️⃣ Support third
4️⃣ Management last

✅ Skills
✅ Culture fit
✅ Growth potential
✅ Coachability

The process:
📋 Clear job description
📋 Structured interviews
📋 Skills assessment
📋 Reference checks

Onboarding:
➡️ Clear expectations
➡️ Training plan
➡️ 30/60/90 days
➡️ Regular check-ins
