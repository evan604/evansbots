# Why Your Advisory Board Should Include Someone 20 Years Younger

## Blog Post - Draft

### Hook
Your peers give you validation. Your younger advisors give you a future. Here's why you need both.

### Outline

**1. The Echo Chamber Problem**
- Advisors who agree with you
- Same experiences, same blind spots
- Comfortable but limiting

**2. The Younger Perspective**
- Seeing technology differently
- Not jaded by "how it used to be"
- Understanding the next generation of customers/employees

**3. What a 20-Year-Younger Advisor Brings**
- Cultural fluency
- Technology optimism
- Different risk calculus
- Future customer insights

**4. How to Find Them**
- Junior employees at portfolio companies
- Young founders in your space
- Recent MBA graduates
- Anyone who's building what you're not

**5. The Rule**
- Don't mentor them, let them mentor you
- Humility over experience
