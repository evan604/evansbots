# The $10M Question: What Are You Actually Optimizing For?

## Blog Post - Draft

### Hook
Most entrepreneurs hit a number and don't know what comes next. Here's how to clarity what you're actually building toward.

### Outline

**1. The Empty Milestone**
- Hit 7 figures, felt nothing
- Hit 8 figures, still nothing
- The money wasn't the point

**2. The Real Questions**
- What do you want your mornings to look like?
- Who do you want to have dinner with?
- What problem are you solving that matters?
- How many more years do you want to do this?

**3. The Alignment Framework**
- Money → Freedom → Impact → Legacy
- Most people skip to money and miss the rest

**4. My Answer**
- Not more money
- Optionality + Impact
- Morning meetings optional
- Working on things that matter
