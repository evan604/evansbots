# The Wealth Manager Your Business Deserves

## Blog Post (Highland) - Draft

### Hook
Most wealth managers serve investors. Not business owners. Here's what separates the ones who actually help from the ones who just take fees.

### Outline

**1. The Problem**
- Most advisors = investors
- Business owners = different
- Different needs
- Different advice

**2. What Business Owners Need**
- Business + personal planning
- Cash flow + investments
- Exit strategy + wealth
- Tax optimization + growth

**3. The Advisors Who Get It**
- Entrepreneur experience
- Holistic view
- Multi-disciplinary
- Fee transparent

**4. Red Flags**
- No business experience
- Only talks investments
- Hidden fees
- One-size-fits-all

**5. The Better Way**
- Integrated planning
- Business + personal
- Aligned incentives
