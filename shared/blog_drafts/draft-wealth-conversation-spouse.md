# The Wealth Conversation You're Not Having With Your Spouse

## Blog Post - Draft

### Hook
Most couples avoid the "what if" conversations until it's too late. Here's how to have them now.

### Outline

**1. The Uncomfortable Truth**
- 90% of couples don't discuss:
  - What happens if one of us can't work?
  - What does retirement actually look like?
  - What are we optimizing for?
- Avoidance feels safe, it's not

**2. The Questions**
- If I died tomorrow, what would you do?
- What does our ideal life look like in 10 years?
- What are you worried about financially?
- What's one thing we should change but haven't?

**3. The Framework**
- Monthly money dates
- No judgment zone
- Separate dreams from deadlines

**4. What Gets Avoided**
- Different risk tolerances
- Different timelines
- Family money dynamics
- Giving money to kids vs not
