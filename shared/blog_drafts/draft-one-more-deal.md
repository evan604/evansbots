# The Hidden Cost of "Just One More Deal"

## Blog Post - Draft

### Hook
You keep saying yes to one more deal. Here's what's actually costing you.

### Outline

**1. The Deal Trap**
- "Just one more" becomes five more
- Each deal has overhead cost
- Not just money - energy, attention, relationships

**2. The Real Costs**
- Opportunity cost (what else you could do)
- Cognitive load
- Family time
- Health
- Reputation (quality suffers)

**3. The Math**
- 10 deals at 80% = 800
- 5 deals at 100% = 500
- But 800 < what you're actually giving up

**4. When to Say No**
- If it takes you away from your family
- If you can't sleep at night
- If it dilutes your focus
- If it's just for ego

**5. The Alternative**
- Less is more
- Say no to good opportunities for great ones
