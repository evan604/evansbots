# The 3-Hour Workday

## Blog Post (Evan Personal) - Draft

### Hook
With AI handling the grunt work, I only work 3 hours a day. Here's my schedule — and why I'm more productive than ever.

### Outline

**1. The Old Way**
- 8-10 hour days
- Busy = productive
- Everything manually
- No systems

**2. The New Way**

- Maximum output

**3. My Schedule**
- Morning: Deep work (1 hour)
- Midday: Decisions (1 hour)
- Afternoon: Review (1 hour)

**4. What AI handles**
- Research
- Drafting
- Scheduling
- Follow-ups
- Data整理

**5. The lesson**
- Busy ≠ productive
- Focus > hours
- Systems over struggle
