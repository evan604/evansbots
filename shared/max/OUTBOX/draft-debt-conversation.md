# The Debt Conversation

## Blog Post (Highland) - Draft

### Hook
Not all debt is bad. Here's how to think about debt as a business owner.

### Outline

**1. Good Debt vs Bad Debt**
- Growth vs consumption
- ROI vs expense
- Leveraged vs broke

**2. Types of Business Debt**
- Term loans
- Lines of credit
- Merchant cash advance
- Equipment financing

**3. When to Use Debt**
- Growth opportunities
- Equipment purchases
- Working capital
- Acquisitions

**4. When to Avoid**
- Lifestyle purchases
- Covering losses
- No ROI
- desperation

**5. The Rules**
- Have a plan
- Know the cost
- Don't over-leverage
- Have exit strategy
