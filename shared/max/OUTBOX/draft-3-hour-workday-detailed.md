# The 3-Hour Workday (v2 - More Detailed)

## Blog Post (Highland) - Draft

### Hook
Forget 80-hour weeks. The most productive people work less. Here's how to do more in 3 hours than most do in 8.

### Outline

**1. The Myth of Busy**
- Long hours ≠ productivity
- Fatigue kills output
- Parkinson's Law: work expands to fill time

**2. The New Reality**
- AI handles execution
- You handle strategy
- Focus beats duration

**3. My 3-Hour Framework**
- Morning (1hr): Deep work - strategy, big decisions
- Midday (1hr): Communication - direction, approvals
- Afternoon (1hr): Review - progress, planning

**4. What AI Handles**
- Research and analysis
- Drafting and editing
- Scheduling and follow-ups
- Data compilation

**5. The Results**
- More done, less time
- Better decisions
- Less burnout
- Actually enjoy work
