# The $10M Question (v2 - More Detailed)

## Blog Post (Highland) - Draft

### Hook
At $10M in revenue, everything changes. You can sell, delegate, leverage your time, or keep building. Here's how to make that choice.

### Outline

**1. The $10M Milestone**
- Psychological barrier
- Operational complexity
- Value creation opportunity
- Exit possibility

**2. What's Different at $10M**
- Multiple offers possible
- Investors interested
- Talent attracted
- Strategic options open

**3. The Four Paths**
- SELL: Take money, exit
- DELEGATE: Hire exec team, step back
- LEVERAGE: Use equity, acquire
- BUILD: Keep growing, scale

**4. How to Decide**
- Your goals?
- Your energy?
- Your market?
- Your team?

**5. The Best Path**
- It's personal
- No right answer
- Know yourself

## LinkedIn Post

**Headline:** The $10M Question — Sell, Delegate, Leverage, or Build?

**Body:**

At $10M in revenue, everything changes.

You can sell, delegate, leverage your time, or keep building. Here's how to make that choice.

What's different at $10M:
📊 Multiple offers become possible
📊 Investors interested
📊 Talent attracted
📊 Strategic options open

The four paths:

1. SELL
Take money, exit
✅ Cash out, move on
❌ Build something else

2. DELEGATE
Hire exec team, step back
✅ Freedom, still owner
❌ Less control

3. LEVERAGE
Use equity, acquire
✅ Scale faster
❌ More complexity

4. BUILD
Keep growing, scale
✅ Biggest upside
❌ Most work

How to decide:
- Your goals?
- Your energy?
- Your market?
- Your team?

The best path?

It's personal. No right answer. Know yourself.

That's the real question.
