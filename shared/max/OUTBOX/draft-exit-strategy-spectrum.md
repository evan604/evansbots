# The Exit Strategy Spectrum

## Blog Post (Highland) - Draft

### Hook
Not everyone wants to sell. Here's the full spectrum of exit strategies for business owners.

### Outline

**1. Full Sale**
- Sell to third party
- Maximum value
- Complete exit

**2. Management Buyout**
- Sell to team
- Continuity
- Partial exit

**3. Family Transfer**
- Pass to kids
- Keep in family
- Long-term

**4. Merger**
- Combine with another
- Synergy play
- Partial ownership

**5. Liquidation**
- Close and sell assets
- Last resort
- Lowest value
