# Buy, Build, or Franchise? The 2026 Entrepreneur's Dilemma

## Blog Post (Highland) - Draft

### Hook
Start from scratch? Buy an existing business? Or franchise? The 2026 answer isn't obvious. Here's how to think about it.

### Outline

**1. The Three Paths**
- Buy: Instant revenue, instant problems
- Build: Slow, but you control everything
- Franchise: Proven system, but less flexibility

**2. When to BUY**
- Cash flow matters now
- You want existing customers
- Industry experience
- Need immediate scale

**3. When to BUILD**
- You have time to wait
- Control matters most
- Unique vision or market
- Low capital

**4. When to FRANCHISE**
- You want proven playbooks
- Support matters
- Brand recognition helps
- Less hands-on operational control

**5. 2026 specifically**
- Buying is cheaper (valuation correction)
- Building has lower cost of entry
- Franchising more competitive

## LinkedIn Post

**Headline:** Buy, Build, or Franchise? The 2026 Entrepreneur's Dilemma

**Body:**

Start from scratch? Buy an existing business? Or franchise?

The 2026 answer isn't obvious. Here's how to think about it.

🛒 BUY
- Instant revenue, instant customers
- You inherit problems too
- Good when: cash flow matters NOW
- Risk: what made it for sale?

🏗️ BUILD
- Slow but you control everything
- Lower capital required
- Good when: you have unique vision
- Risk: time to profitability

📋 FRANCHISE
- Proven system, proven playbook
- Support included
- Good when: you want guidance
- Risk: less flexibility, ongoing fees

2026 specifically:
- Buying is cheaper (valuation correction from 2024-25)
- Building has lowest cost of entry
- Franchising more competitive (more players)

The question isn't which path is "best."

It's which is best for YOUR situation, capital, timeline, and goals.

What works for one entrepreneur fails for another.
