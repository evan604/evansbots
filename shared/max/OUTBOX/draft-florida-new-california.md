# Florida Is the New California

## Blog Post (Highland) - Draft

### Hook
Tech founders are fleeing California for Florida. $500M+ in startup wealth moved in the last 2 years alone. Here's why.

### Outline

**1. The Exodus**
- No state income tax
- Lower cost of living
- Business-friendly
- Quality of life

**2. The Numbers**
- $500M+ in wealth moved to FL
- Many more on the way
- Companies relocating HQ

**3. Why Florida specifically**
- No income tax
- Good airports (Orlando, Tampa)
- Weather
- Growing tech scene

**4. The opportunity**
- Network effects
- Community building
- First mover advantage

**5. But it's not for everyone**
- Hurricanes
- Insurance costs
- Different pace
