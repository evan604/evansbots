# The Insurance Checklist for Business Owners

## Blog Post (Highland) - Draft

### Hook
Are you properly insured? Here's the checklist every business owner needs.

### Outline

**1. Business Insurance**
- General liability
- Professional liability
- Property insurance
- Business interruption

**2. Health Insurance**
- Group plan
- Individual
- HSA

**3. Life & Disability**
- Key person
- Buy-sell
- Your coverage

**4. Liability**
- Umbrella
- Directors & officers
- Cyber insurance

**5. Review Regularly**
- Annual check
- Life changes
- Coverage gaps
