# Why Business Owners Need a Different Kind of Advisor

## Blog Post (Highland) - Draft

### Hook
Your cousin's stockbroker isn't equipped to help you sell your business. Here's why business owners need a different kind of advisor.

### Outline

**1. The Mismatch**
- Advisors trained for investors
- Not for entrepreneurs
- Different financial picture
- Different needs

**2. What Business Owners Face**
- Exit planning
- Business valuation
- Tax optimization
- Succession
- Cash flow

**3. Generic Advice Fails**
- "Diversify into stocks"
- "Buy index funds"
- Doesn't apply
- Missed opportunities

**4. What You Need**
- Business expertise
- Exit experience
- Tax knowledge
- Network of buyers

**5. The Right Advisor**
- Has been there
- Knows the game
- Adds real value
