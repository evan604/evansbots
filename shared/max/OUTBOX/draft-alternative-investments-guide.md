# The Business Owner's Guide to Alternative Investments

## Blog Post (Highland) - Draft

### Hook
Stocks and bonds aren't enough anymore. Here's what smart business owners are adding to their portfolios.

### Outline

**1. Beyond Traditional**
- Stocks + bonds = traditional
- Not enough diversification
- Lower returns
- More volatility

**2. Alternative Investments**
- Private equity
- Private credit
- Real estate
- Commodities

**3. Private Equity**
- Ownership in private companies
- Higher returns
- Less liquidity
- Long horizon

**4. Private Credit**
- Lending, not equity
- 8-12% yields
- Senior position
- Lower risk

**5. How to Get Started**
- Funds vs direct
- Minimums
- Due diligence
