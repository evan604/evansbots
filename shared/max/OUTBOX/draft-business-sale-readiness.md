# The Business Sale Readiness Checklist

## Blog Post (Highland) - Draft

### Hook
Ready to sell? Here's the checklist that determines if your business is ready for buyers.

### Outline

**1. Financial Cleanliness**
- Clean books
- Verified revenue
- Clear expenses
- Audit ready

**2. Operations**
- Systems in place
- Processes documented
- Key person dependency
- Customer concentration

**3. Legal**
- Contracts in order
- IP protected
- Employee issues resolved
- Litigation free

**4. Growth**
- Recurring revenue
- Growth trajectory
- Market position
- Competitive advantage

**5. The Deal Terms**
- Price expectation
- Financing ready
- Transition plan
- Non-competes

### Word count target: 800-1000 words
