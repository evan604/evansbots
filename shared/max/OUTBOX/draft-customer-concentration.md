# The Customer Concentration Problem

## Blog Post (Highland) - Draft

### Hook
One big customer taking up 80% of revenue? That's a business risk. Here's what to do about it.

### Outline

**1. The Risk**
- Customer dependency
- Single point of failure
- Negotiation weakness

**2. The Signs**
- One customer > 30%
- Long contracts
- Relationship reliance

**3. The Fix**
- Diversify
- Expand markets
- Upsell existing
- Add product lines

**4. The Timeline**
- 6 months: Plan
- 12 months: Execute
- 24 months: Diversified

**5. The Exit Impact**
- Buyers hate concentration
- Reduces valuation
- Increases risk
