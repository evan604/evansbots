## LinkedIn Post

**Headline:** The $10M Question: Are You Working IN or ON Your Business?

**Body:**

At what point do you stop working IN your business?

And start working ON it?

That's the $10M question every entrepreneur faces.

Here's the progression:

📊 $0-500K: Survival mode — you do everything
📊 $500K-2M: Growth mode — you're scaling
📊 $2M-10M: The transition — systems matter
📊 $10M+: Ownership mode — you can exit

At $10M, you can:
- Sell the business
- Delegate operations
- Leverage your time
- Actually exit

But here's the problem:

Most owners stay stuck in operations.

They can't let go.
The business depends on them.
No exit ready.

The solution?

Start thinking like an owner.
Build systems that work without you.
Hire managers who can run things.
Work ON the business, not IN it.

The question isn't how much you make.

It's: Are you working IN or ON your business?
