# Post Drafts for Evan

## Draft 1: Voice Agents

The best voice AI isn't the one that sounds most human. It's the one that knows when to shut up.

Most voice assistants fail because they treat every silence like a failure. Real conversation has pauses. It has thinking time.

The future isn't faster responses. It's smarter ones.

---

## Draft 2: Productivity

Hot take: The 2-minute rule is backwards.

If something takes <2 minutes, just do it. But here's what nobody mentions — most "2 minute" tasks aren't worth your attention at all.

They're quick dopamine hits that keep you busy but not productive.

Batch the small stuff. Protect the deep work. That's it.

---

## Draft 3: AI Agents

Most "AI agents" aren't agents. They're just fancy if-then chains with a chat interface.

Real agents:
- Make decisions with incomplete info
- Know when to ask vs. assume
- Admit when they're wrong

Everything else is a chatbot with a resume.
