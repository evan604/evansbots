# The CEO's New Best Friend

## Blog Post (Highland) - Draft

### Hook
Your AI doesn't sleep. Doesn't complain. Doesn't quit. But can it replace your team? The answer might surprise you.

### Outline

**1. The Promise**
- 24/7 availability
- Infinite patience
- No ego
- Instant recall

**2. The Reality**
- It hallucinates
- It lacks judgment
- It can't read rooms
- It needs humans

**3. The Sweet Spot**
- AI for execution
- Humans for strategy
- Together: superpower

**4. The New Model**
- CEO + AI agents = lean team
- Humans for what matters
- AI for everything else

**5. The Question**
- Not "AI or humans"
- But "AI AND humans — in what order?"
