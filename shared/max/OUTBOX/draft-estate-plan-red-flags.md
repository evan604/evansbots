**1. No Plan**

- No trust
- State decides

- New laws
- Life changes

**3. Missing Pieces**
- No healthcare directive
- No power of attorney
- No business succession

**4. Tax Inefficiencies**
- No planning
- Missed credits
- State taxes

**5. The Fix**
- Review annually

- Update regularly
