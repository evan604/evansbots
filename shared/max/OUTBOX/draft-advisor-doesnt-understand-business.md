# Why Your Financial Advisor Doesn't Understand Your Business

## Blog Post (Highland) - Draft

### Hook
Your financial advisor manages your money. But do they understand your business? Most don't. Here's why that matters.

### Outline

**1. The Gap**
- Advisors trained on markets
- Not on entrepreneurship
- Different worldviews

**2. What Business Owners Need**
- Cash flow management
- Business valuation
- Exit planning
- Tax optimization

**3. The Problem**
- Generic advice
- Doesn't fit
- Missed opportunities

**4. The Solution**
- Advisors who get business
- Or advisors + business consultants
- Holistic approach

**5. The Question**
- Does your advisor understand your business?

### Word count target: 800-1000 words
