# The New Wealth Advisors

## Blog Post (Highland) - Draft

### Hook
AI is coming for financial advisors. The question isn't if — it's who survives. Here's what the new wealth advisor looks like.

### Outline

**1. The Disruption**
- AI does analysis faster
- Lower costs
- 24/7 availability
- No human bias

**2. The Advisors at Risk**
- Transaction-based
- Product-pushers
- No tech stack
- Commodity services

**3. The Advisors Who Survive**
- Relationship-focused
- Holistic advisors
- Tech-enabled
- Value-added

**4. The New Model**
- AI handles data
- Humans handle trust
- Combined = unstoppable

**5. The Opportunity**
- Be early
- Adopt AI
- Position differently
