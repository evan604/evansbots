# AI Executive Command Reference
## Quick Cheat Sheet for Daily Use

---

## Daily Briefing

| You Say | AI Does |
|---------|---------|
| "Good morning" | Sends morning briefing with weather, calendar, tasks |
| "What's my day?" | Lists today's events and priorities |
| "Weather today?" | Checks and reports weather for your location |
| "What's on my calendar?" | Reads today's calendar events |
| "What's happening this week?" | Summarizes upcoming week's schedule |

---

## Task Management

| You Say | AI Does |
|---------|---------|
| "Remind me to [task] tomorrow at 3pm" | Creates calendar reminder |
| "What's on my todo list?" | Shows current tasks |
| "Add to my tasks: [new task]" | Appends to todo.md |
| "Mark [task] complete" | Checks off task |
| "What did I say I'd do?" | Searches memory for commitments |
| "Follow up on [thing]" | Logs follow-up in tasks |

## Tips

**1. Be specific:** "Remind me to call Sarah about the deal Friday at 2pm" works better than "remind me about Sarah"

**2. Context helps:** "Search for competitors of [company]" gives better results than "search for competitors"

**3. Follow-ups:** I remember what you say. "What was that thing from Tuesday?" — I check memory.

**4. Files:** Send me images, PDFs, ZIPs. I can analyze them.

**5. Heartbeat windows:** I check HEARTBEAT.md automatically. If it's quiet hours, I might skip non-urgent updates.

---

## Quick Reminder

When in doubt, just **say what you want**. I understand plain language.

**Examples:**
- "I'm stressed, help me prioritize"
- "What's the weather like for my run?"
- "Summarize the last hour of our chat"
- "Find that file I sent about taxes"
- "Draft a follow-up to the investor meeting"

---

*Keep this handy. The more you use it, the more natural it becomes.*
